Aloo-Parantha

Description

Stuffed flatbread made with whole wheat flour, filled with spiced mashed potatoes, rolled thin, cooked on a griddle with oil or ghee until golden brown, and served hot with yogurt or pickles.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Filling:

  • Potatoes (boiled, peeled, and mashed)
  • Green chilies (finely chopped)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (cilantro), chopped
  • Cumin seeds
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare dough: Mix whole wheat flour, salt, water; knead smooth, rest 15-20 min.
  2. Prepare filling: Combine mashed potatoes, chilies, coriander, cumin, chili powder, salt.
  3. Assemble: Divide dough, roll into 4-5" circle, add filling, seal edges, flatten, roll to 7-8" circle.
  4. Cook: Heat griddle, cook paratha with oil/ghee until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with yogurt, pickles, or chutney.
