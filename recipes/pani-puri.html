Pani-Puri

Description

Pani-Puri is a combination of round crunchy puri, with variation of fillings to choose from and flavored water.

It is eaten in one bite to experience the combination of all flavors and textures at once,making it a refreshing snack.

Ingredients

For Puris:

  • Semolina/Wheat Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Oil for deep frying
  • all-purpose flour
  • Water for kneading dough

For Pani:

  • Mint Leaves
  • Corainder Leaves
  • Green Chillies
  • Black Salt
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Ginger

For Fillings:

  • Boiled and mashed potatoes
  • Boiled Chickpeas
  • Black Salt
  • Tamarind Chutney
Note:Pani-Puri has variety of different recipe

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough : Mix Semolina ,all-purpose flour,Baking Soda and Salt in a bowl.
  2. Gradually add water and knead into stiff yet smooth dough.
  3. Cover it with damp cloth and let it rest 15 min.
  4. Divide the dough in small lemon sized balls and roll out each ball into a thin circle (2-3 inches diameter) evenly.
  5. Heat Oil in deep frying pan over medium heat.Once Hot gently slide in one rolled out puri.it should puff up immediately.
  6. press lightly in with spatula or spoon in the center to help it puff up evenly & fry until golden brown color and crispy.
  7. Remove oil using slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.Fry all puris in same manner.
  8. Blend mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chilies, and ginger to a smooth paste using little water.
  9. combine the blended green paste, cumin powder, black salt, regular salt, and chilled water.Mix well until all the flavors are combined
  10. refrigerate the pani for atleast 30 min to allow flavors to meld together(recommend to prepare when dough is kept to rest to save time)
  11. Boil the chickpeas, potatoes until they are fully cooked. Peel and mash potatoes in a bowl.
  12. combine mashed potatoes, boiled chickpeas, tamarind chutney and black salt.
  13. Take small amount of fillings and pani in bowls and a plate with puris. Pick a puri and smash a hole on top and fill fillings and dip the puri inside bowl containing the flavored pani.Eat in one bite!
